Hive Notes is publicly accessible as a login-protected application.

## Overview

The visible landing experience emphasizes authenticated access before any workspace or note content is shown.

## Visible Public Experience

The public-facing page currently shows:

- Hive Notes branding
- "Log into your account" heading
- Username field
- Password field
- Sign in action

## Intended Users

- Users who require account-protected access to application content
- Individuals or teams adopting authenticated note workflows
