GMB Dashboard is a local SEO product focused on Google Business Profile performance tracking and campaign planning.

## Overview

The product combines rank visibility, keyword tracking, and seasonal demand modeling to help teams prioritize local SEO actions month by month.

## Core Capabilities

- Advanced GMB tooling for keyword and ranking visibility
- Seasonal campaign planner with month-by-month volume shifts
- Annual trend overview across all four seasons
- Smart recommendations for next-step campaign actions
- Dashboard indicators such as GBP score, average rank, and top rankings

## Notable On-Page Signals

- Seasonal planning model shows peak demand in July (+35 percent) and lower demand in December (-20 percent)
- Example dashboard indicators include GBP Score 54, 6 tracked keywords, and Average Rank #8
- Recommendation engine highlights action items like seasonal posts, bid adjustments, and landing page updates

## Intended Users

- Local businesses optimizing Google Business Profile visibility
- Agencies managing multi-month local SEO strategies
- Growth teams planning demand-aware budget allocation
